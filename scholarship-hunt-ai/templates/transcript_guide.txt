ACADEMIC TRANSCRIPT PREPARATION GUIDE

1. WHAT IS AN ACADEMIC TRANSCRIPT?
An academic transcript is an official document from your university that lists all the courses you have taken, the grades you received, and your final CGPA or degree classification.

2. HOW TO OBTAIN IT:
- You must request an "Official Transcript" from your university's administrative or registrar office.
- Some universities offer digital transcripts (e-transcripts) which are generally accepted globally.
- Ensure the transcript is in English. If the original is in your national language, you must get it officially translated and notarized.

3. KEY CHECKLIST BEFORE ATTACHING:
- [ ] Stamped and signed by the university registrar.
- [ ] Contains grading scale explanation (usually on the back page).
- [ ] Crisp, clear, colored scan (Save as a single PDF if multiple pages).
- [ ] File size is optimized (usually under 2MB).

Do not submit student portal screenshots unless the application explicitly allows "Unofficial Transcripts" for the initial phase.
