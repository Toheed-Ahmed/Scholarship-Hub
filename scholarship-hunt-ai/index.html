Discovery

Scholarship Matcher

Find matching scholarships based on your profile.

"Act as a university admissions counselor. I am looking for fully-funded master's scholarships in [Field] in [Country]. My GPA is [GPA]. Provide a list of 5 high-probability scholarships with links."

SOP Reviewer

Get rigorous feedback on your Statement of Purpose.

"Act as a strict admissions committee member for a top [Field] program. Review my Statement of Purpose below. Point out weak arguments, suggest structural improvements, and rate it out of 10. \n\n[Paste SOP]"
Outreach

Professor Cold Email

Craft a compelling email for supervision.

"Help me write a persuasive 150-word cold email to Professor [Name] at [University]. Their recent paper on [Topic] fascinated me. I want to convey my interest in joining their lab as a PhD student."
